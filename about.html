Introduction

I'm Aaron Doucett, a geospatial professional and creative problem solver with a background in GIS, earth observation, and data-driven storytelling. Over the past decade, I've worked on everything from climate research and urban planning to developing marketing strategies for cutting-edge geospatial tech. When I'm not exploring new ways to visualize complex data, you can find me logging miles on the roads training for marathons, cooking up something delicious in the kitchen, or capturing moments behind the camera. I’ve built this site to share my work, passions, and the projects that keep me inspired. Thanks for visiting—take a look around, and feel free to connect!
