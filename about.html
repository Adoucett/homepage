Welcome


Introduction


I'm Aaron Doucett, a geospatial professional and creative problem solver with a background in GIS, earth observation, and data-driven storytelling. Over the past decade, I've worked on everything from climate research and urban planning to developing marketing strategies for cutting-edge geospatial tech. I’ve built this site to share my work, passions, and the projects that keep me inspired. Thanks for visiting—take a look around, and feel free to connect!


Biography

I grew up in Western Massachusetts and moved to the greater Boston area after completing an Undergraduate degree at UMass Amherst.

In my free time, I enjoy playing jazz piano, photography, cooking, and pursing creative projects.

After graduating, I grew to become an avid cyclist and spent a lot of time exploring the roads of Massachusetts

As a runner, I have enjoyed pushing myself with various race distances ranging from 5KM to the marathon.

In 2024 I ran the Boston, Chicago and Indianapolis Marathons, setting a new personal best time of 2:52:16 in Indianapolis.
